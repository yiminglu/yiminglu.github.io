Thought

Not a good ending.

People always do the same thing. And one always wishes when the last time he does this thing, it could be a good ending.

I’ve done one thing for many times. It is is also suitable for me.

However, I’ve got a not-so-good ending. What should I say? I’ve believed there should be no more this kind of stuffs bothering me after I’ve finished the second-last time. It’s very good, I’ve already believed it would be my last time. Then I got the message that I had to do it again, and I knew it would not be declined.

Then, I got a bitter end. I have no one to complain about this. Sorry for me, and sorry for them. Shame on the bitter ending.
